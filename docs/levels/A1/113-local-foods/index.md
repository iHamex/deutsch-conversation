# Regionale Lebensmittel / Local Foods

---

## 001: Regionale Produkte / Regional Products

**Situation:**  
Anna fragt nach regionalen Produkten.  
*Anna asks about regional products.*

### Dialog / Dialogue

**Anna:**  
Haben Sie regionale Produkte?  
*Do you have regional products?*

**Verkäufer:**  
Ja, wir haben viele regionale Produkte.  
*Yes, we have many regional products.*

**Anna:**  
Welche Produkte haben Sie?  
*Which products do you have?*

**Verkäufer:**  
Wir haben regionales Gemüse und Obst.  
*We have regional vegetables and fruit.*

**Anna:**  
Woher kommt das Gemüse?  
*Where does the vegetables come from?*

**Verkäufer:**  
Aus der Region.  
*From the region.*

**Anna:**  
Das ist gut.  
*That's good.*

**Verkäufer:**  
Ja, es ist frisch.  
*Yes, it's fresh.*

**Anna:**  
Ich nehme regionales Gemüse.  
*I'll take regional vegetables.*

## Grammatik / Grammar

### Asking "Where from" with "woher kommt"

**English:** To ask where local or regional products come from, use "woher kommt" (where does ... come from) followed by the item. This is essential when buying local foods.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Woher kommt das Gemüse?** - Where does the vegetables come from?
- **Woher kommt der Käse?** - Where does the cheese come from?
- **Woher kommen die Eier?** - Where do the eggs come from?
- **Aus der Region** - From the region

### Asking About Availability with "haben Sie"

**English:** To ask if a store has regional or local products, use "haben Sie" (do you have) followed by the item. The answer confirms availability.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Haben Sie regionale Produkte?** - Do you have regional products?
- **Haben Sie lokalen Käse?** - Do you have local cheese?
- **Ja, wir haben viele regionale Produkte** - Yes, we have many regional products
- **Ja, hier ist lokaler Käse** - Yes, here is local cheese

### Expressing Origin with "von" or "aus"

**English:** To express where products come from, use "von" (from) for places like farms or businesses, or "aus" (from) for regions or areas.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Von einer lokalen Käserei** - From a local dairy
- **Von einem Bauernhof in der Nähe** - From a farm nearby
- **Aus der Region** - From the region
- **Von einem lokalen Imker** - From a local beekeeper

### Asking About Quality with "ist es frisch"

**English:** To ask if regional products are fresh, use "ist es frisch" (is it fresh) or "sind sie frisch" (are they fresh). Local products are often valued for freshness.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ist es frisch?** - Is it fresh?
- **Sind sie frisch?** - Are they fresh?
- **Ja, es ist ganz frisch** - Yes, it's very fresh
- **Ja, sehr frisch** - Yes, very fresh
