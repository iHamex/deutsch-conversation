# Konserven / Canned Foods

---

## 001: Dosenbohnen kaufen / Buying Canned Beans

**Situation:**  
Anna kauft Dosenbohnen.  
*Anna buys canned beans.*

### Dialog / Dialogue

**Anna:**  
Entschuldigung, wo sind die Dosenbohnen?  
*Excuse me, where are the canned beans?*

**Verkäufer:**  
Die Dosenbohnen sind in Regal drei.  
*The canned beans are in aisle three.*

**Anna:**  
Danke!  
*Thanks!*

**Anna:**  
Welche Bohnen haben Sie?  
*Which beans do you have?*

**Verkäufer:**  
Wir haben weiße Bohnen, rote Bohnen und Kidneybohnen.  
*We have white beans, red beans and kidney beans.*

**Anna:**  
Ich nehme weiße Bohnen.  
*I'll take white beans.*

**Verkäufer:**  
Wie viele Dosen?  
*How many cans?*

**Anna:**  
Zwei Dosen, bitte.  
*Two cans, please.*

## Grammatik / Grammar

### Asking "Where" with "wo"

**English:** To ask where canned foods are located in a store, use "wo" (where) followed by "sind" (are) for plural items.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Wo sind die Dosenbohnen?** - Where are the canned beans?
- **Die Dosenbohnen sind in Regal drei** - The canned beans are in aisle three
- **Hier sind Dosentomaten** - Here are canned tomatoes
- **Hier ist Dosenmais** - Here is canned corn

### Asking About Varieties with "welche Sorten"

**English:** To ask about different varieties of canned foods, use "welche Sorten haben Sie" (which varieties do you have) or ask about specific types like "welche Bohnen" (which beans).

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Welche Sorten haben Sie?** - Which varieties do you have?
- **Welche Bohnen haben Sie?** - Which beans do you have?
- **Welche Früchte haben Sie?** - Which fruits do you have?
- **Wir haben ganze Tomaten und gehackte Tomaten** - We have whole tomatoes and chopped tomatoes

### Asking About Use with "wofür verwendet man" or "wie verwendet man"

**English:** To ask what canned foods are used for or how to use them, use "wofür verwendet man" (what do you use ... for) or "wie verwendet man" (how do you use).

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Wofür verwendet man sie?** - What do you use it for?
- **Wie verwendet man sie?** - How do you use it?
- **Für Kuchen oder als Dessert** - For cake or as dessert
- **Für Kaffee oder zum Kochen** - For coffee or for cooking

### Expressing Need with "ich brauche"

**English:** To express that you need specific canned foods, use "ich brauche" (I need) followed by the items. This is useful when shopping for multiple items.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ich brauche verschiedene Konserven** - I need different canned foods
- **Welche Konserven möchten Sie?** - Which canned foods would you like?
- **Tomaten, Bohnen und Mais** - Tomatoes, beans and corn
- **Ich nehme zwei Dosen** - I'll take two cans
