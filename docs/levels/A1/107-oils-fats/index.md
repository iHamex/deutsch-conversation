# Öle und Fette / Oils and Fats

---

## 001: Olivenöl kaufen / Buying Olive Oil

**Situation:**  
Anna kauft Olivenöl.  
*Anna buys olive oil.*

### Dialog / Dialogue

**Anna:**  
Entschuldigung, wo ist das Olivenöl?  
*Excuse me, where is the olive oil?*

**Verkäufer:**  
Das Olivenöl ist in Regal drei.  
*The olive oil is in aisle three.*

**Anna:**  
Danke!  
*Thanks!*

**Anna:**  
Welche Sorten haben Sie?  
*Which varieties do you have?*

**Verkäufer:**  
Wir haben natives und raffiniertes Olivenöl.  
*We have extra virgin and refined olive oil.*

**Anna:**  
Was ist der Unterschied?  
*What is the difference?*

**Verkäufer:**  
Natives Öl ist besser für Salat.  
*Extra virgin oil is better for salad.*

**Anna:**  
Ich nehme natives Olivenöl.  
*I'll take extra virgin olive oil.*

## Grammatik / Grammar

### Asking "Where" with "wo"

**English:** To ask where oils and fats are located in a store, use "wo" (where) followed by "ist" (is) for singular items or "sind" (are) for plural items.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Wo ist das Olivenöl?** - Where is the olive oil?
- **Wo ist die Butter?** - Where is the butter?
- **Das Olivenöl ist in Regal drei** - The olive oil is in aisle three
- **Die Butter ist im Kühlregal** - The butter is in the refrigerated section

### Asking About Varieties with "welche Sorten"

**English:** To ask about different varieties of oils or fats, use "welche Sorten haben Sie" (which varieties do you have) or "welche Butter haben Sie" (which butter do you have).

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Welche Sorten haben Sie?** - Which varieties do you have?
- **Welche Butter haben Sie?** - Which butter do you have?
- **Wir haben verschiedene Sorten** - We have different varieties
- **Wir haben normale und gesalzene Butter** - We have normal and salted butter

### Asking "What" with "was ist"

**English:** To ask what something is, use "was ist" (what is) followed by the item. This is useful when learning about unfamiliar oils or fats.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Was ist Rapsöl?** - What is rapeseed oil?
- **Was ist der Unterschied?** - What is the difference?
- **Was ist Schmalz?** - What is lard?
- **Das ist ein Öl aus Raps** - That's an oil made from rapeseed

### Asking About Use with "wofür verwendet man"

**English:** To ask what oils or fats are used for, use "wofür verwendet man" (what do you use ... for) followed by the item. The answer explains the purpose.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Wofür verwendet man es?** - What do you use it for?
- **Zum Braten und Kochen** - For frying and cooking
- **Zum Kochen und Backen** - For cooking and baking
- **Für Salat** - For salad
