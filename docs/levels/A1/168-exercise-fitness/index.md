# Sport und Fitness / Exercise Fitness

---

## 001: Im Fitnessstudio / At Gym

**Situation:**  
Lisa kommt zum ersten Mal ins Fitnessstudio.  
*Lisa comes to the gym for the first time.*

### Dialog / Dialogue

**Angestellter:**  
Guten Tag! Kann ich Ihnen helfen?  
*Good day! Can I help you?*

**Lisa:**  
Guten Tag! Ich möchte trainieren.  
*Good day! I would like to exercise.*

**Angestellter:**  
Sind Sie Mitglied?  
*Are you a member?*

**Lisa:**  
Nein, ich bin kein Mitglied.  
*No, I'm not a member.*

**Angestellter:**  
Dann können Sie eine Tageskarte kaufen.  
*Then you can buy a day pass.*

**Lisa:**  
Wie viel kostet das?  
*How much does that cost?*

**Angestellter:**  
Fünfzehn Euro.  
*Fifteen euros.*

**Lisa:**  
Das ist okay.  
*That's okay.*

## Grammatik / Grammar

### Expressing What You Want to Do with "ich möchte" and "ich gehe"

**English:** When talking about exercise and fitness, use "ich möchte trainieren" (I would like to exercise), "ich gehe laufen" (I'm going running), or "ich mache Yoga" (I do yoga). You can also invite others with "möchtest du" (would you like).

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ich möchte trainieren** - I would like to exercise
- **Ich gehe heute schwimmen** - I'm going swimming today
- **Ich mache Yoga** - I do yoga
- **Möchtest du laufen gehen?** - Would you like to go running?

### Asking "Where" and "When" with "wo" and "wann"

**English:** When planning exercise activities, ask "wo trainieren wir?" (where do we train?), "wo laufen wir?" (where do we run?), or "wann treffen wir uns?" (when do we meet?). Answer with locations like "im Park" (in the park) or "im Schwimmbad" (in the swimming pool), and times like "um drei Uhr" (at three o'clock).

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Wo laufen wir?** - Where do we run?
- **Im Park** - In the park
- **Wann treffen wir uns?** - When do we meet?
- **Um drei Uhr** - At three o'clock

### Asking "How Often" with "wie oft"

**English:** To ask about frequency of exercise, use "wie oft trainieren Sie?" (how often do you train?) or "wie oft machst du Yoga?" (how often do you do yoga?). Answer with "dreimal pro Woche" (three times per week), "zweimal pro Woche" (twice per week), or "täglich" (daily).

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Wie oft machst du Yoga?** - How often do you do yoga?
- **Dreimal pro Woche** - Three times per week
- **Wie oft trainieren Sie?** - How often do you train?
- **Zweimal pro Woche** - Twice per week

### Expressing Goals with "ich möchte werden"

**English:** When talking about fitness goals, use "ich möchte" (I want) followed by "werden" (become) and an adjective like "stärker" (stronger), "fitter" (fitter), or "abnehmen" (lose weight). You can also say "das ist gut für" (that's good for) followed by "die Gesundheit" (health) or "die Muskeln" (the muscles).

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ich möchte stärker werden** - I want to become stronger
- **Ich möchte fitter werden** - I want to become fitter
- **Ich möchte abnehmen** - I want to lose weight
- **Das ist gut für die Gesundheit** - That's good for health
