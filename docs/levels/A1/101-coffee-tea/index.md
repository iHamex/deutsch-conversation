# Kaffee und Tee / Coffee and Tea

---

## 001: Kaffee bestellen / Ordering Coffee

**Situation:**  
Anna bestellt einen Kaffee im Café.  
*Anna orders a coffee at the café.*

### Dialog / Dialogue

**Anna:**  
Ich möchte einen Kaffee, bitte.  
*I would like a coffee, please.*

**Barista:**  
Gerne. Welchen Kaffee möchten Sie?  
*Gladly. Which coffee would you like?*

**Anna:**  
Was haben Sie?  
*What do you have?*

**Barista:**  
Wir haben normalen Kaffee, Espresso und Cappuccino.  
*We have normal coffee, espresso and cappuccino.*

**Anna:**  
Ich nehme einen normalen Kaffee, bitte.  
*I'll take a normal coffee, please.*

**Barista:**  
Sehr gut. Mit Milch und Zucker?  
*Very good. With milk and sugar?*

**Anna:**  
Ja, mit Milch. Zucker brauche ich nicht.  
*Yes, with milk. I don't need sugar.*

**Barista:**  
Das macht zwei Euro fünfzig.  
*That makes two euros fifty.*

## Grammatik / Grammar

### Coffee and Tea Types as Nouns

**English:** All coffee and tea types in German are nouns and are capitalized. They often have specific articles (der/die/das).

**Examples from conversations:**

- **der Kaffee** - the coffee
- **der Cappuccino** - the cappuccino
- **der Espresso** - the espresso
- **der Tee** - the tea

### "mit" and "ohne" for Customization

**English:** "Mit" (with) and "ohne" (without) are essential when customizing coffee and tea orders.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Mit Milch und Zucker** - With milk and sugar
- **Ohne Zitrone** - Without lemon
- **Mit oder ohne Zitrone?** - With or without lemon?

### Size Expressions: "groß" and "klein"

**English:** "Groß" (large) and "klein" (small) are used to specify sizes when ordering coffee drinks.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Groß oder klein?** - Large or small?
- **Groß, bitte** - Large, please
- **Ein großer Cappuccino** - A large cappuccino

### "mögen" for Preferences

**English:** "Mögen" (to like) is used when asking about coffee and tea preferences.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Wie magst du deinen Kaffee?** - How do you like your coffee?
- **Ich mag schwarzen Tee** - I like black tea
- **Magst du Kaffee?** - Do you like coffee?
