# Tiefkühlkost / Frozen Foods

---

## 001: Tiefkühlpizza / Frozen Pizza

**Situation:**  
Anna kauft Tiefkühlpizza.  
*Anna buys frozen pizza.*

### Dialog / Dialogue

**Anna:**  
Entschuldigung, wo ist die Tiefkühlpizza?  
*Excuse me, where is the frozen pizza?*

**Verkäufer:**  
Die Tiefkühlpizza ist in der Tiefkühltruhe.  
*The frozen pizza is in the freezer.*

**Anna:**  
Danke!  
*Thanks!*

**Anna:**  
Welche Sorten haben Sie?  
*Which varieties do you have?*

**Verkäufer:**  
Wir haben verschiedene Sorten.  
*We have different varieties.*

**Anna:**  
Welche ist am besten?  
*Which one is the best?*

**Verkäufer:**  
Diese Pizza ist sehr beliebt.  
*This pizza is very popular.*

**Anna:**  
Ich nehme eine Pizza.  
*I'll take a pizza.*

## Grammatik / Grammar

### Asking "Where" with "wo"

**English:** To ask where frozen foods are located in a store, use "wo" (where) followed by "ist" (is) for singular or "sind" (are) for plural items.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Wo ist die Tiefkühlpizza?** - Where is the frozen pizza?
- **Die Tiefkühlpizza ist in der Tiefkühltruhe** - The frozen pizza is in the freezer
- **Hier ist Tiefkühlgemüse** - Here are frozen vegetables
- **Hier sind Tiefkühlkartoffeln** - Here are frozen potatoes

### Asking About Varieties with "welche Sorten"

**English:** To ask about different varieties of frozen foods, use "welche Sorten haben Sie" (which varieties do you have) or ask about specific types like "welches Gemüse" (which vegetables).

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Welche Sorten haben Sie?** - Which varieties do you have?
- **Welches Gemüse haben Sie?** - Which vegetables do you have?
- **Welche Früchte haben Sie?** - Which fruits do you have?
- **Wir haben verschiedene Sorten** - We have different varieties

### Asking About Use with "wie verwendet man"

**English:** To ask how to use frozen foods, use "wie verwendet man" (how do you use) followed by the item. The answer explains the method.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Wie verwendet man es?** - How do you use it?
- **Man taut es auf oder backt es direkt** - You thaw it or bake it directly
- **Wie verwendet man Tiefkühlbrot?** - How do you use frozen bread?
- **Das ist praktisch** - That's practical

### Expressing Need with "ich brauche"

**English:** To express that you need specific frozen products, use "ich brauche" (I need) followed by the items. This is useful when shopping for multiple items.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ich brauche verschiedene Tiefkühlprodukte** - I need different frozen products
- **Welche Produkte möchten Sie?** - Which products would you like?
- **Pizza, Gemüse und Obst** - Pizza, vegetables and fruit
- **Ich nehme zwei Packungen Erbsen** - I'll take two packages of peas
