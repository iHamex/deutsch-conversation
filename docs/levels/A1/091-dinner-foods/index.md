# Abendessen / Dinner Foods

---

## 001: Fisch zum Abendessen / Fish for Dinner

**Situation:**  
Anna bestellt Fisch zum Abendessen im Restaurant.  
*Anna orders fish for dinner at the restaurant.*

### Dialog / Dialogue

**Kellner:**  
Was möchten Sie zum Abendessen?  
*What would you like for dinner?*

**Anna:**  
Ich möchte Fisch, bitte.  
*I would like fish, please.*

**Kellner:**  
Gerne. Welchen Fisch möchten Sie?  
*Gladly. Which fish would you like?*

**Anna:**  
Was haben Sie?  
*What do you have?*

**Kellner:**  
Wir haben Lachs und Forelle.  
*We have salmon and trout.*

**Anna:**  
Ich nehme den Lachs, bitte.  
*I'll take the salmon, please.*

**Kellner:**  
Sehr gut. Möchten Sie eine Beilage?  
*Very good. Would you like a side dish?*

**Anna:**  
Ja, bitte Kartoffeln und Gemüse.  
*Yes, please potatoes and vegetables.*

**Kellner:**  
Gut. Kommt in zwanzig Minuten.  
*Good. Coming in twenty minutes.*

## Grammatik / Grammar

### "zum Abendessen" (for dinner)

**English:** "Zum Abendessen" means "for dinner" and is used to specify when you eat something.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Was möchten Sie zum Abendessen?** - What would you like for dinner?
- **Ich esse Fisch zum Abendessen** - I eat fish for dinner
- **Zum Abendessen esse ich Pasta** - For dinner I eat pasta

### "wie" for Asking How Food Should Be Prepared

**English:** "Wie" (how) is used to ask how food should be prepared, especially for steaks and other meats.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Wie möchten Sie das Steak?** - How would you like the steak?
- **Mittel, bitte** - Medium, please
- **Wie soll ich das machen?** - How should I make that?

### Present Tense of "möchten" and "essen"

**English:** "Möchten" (would like) and "essen" (to eat) are essential verbs when talking about dinner foods.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ich möchte ein Steak** - I would like a steak
- **Ich esse zu Abend** - I eat dinner
- **Was möchten Sie?** - What would you like?

### Time Expressions with Dinner

**English:** Time expressions are often used when talking about dinner, especially "spät" (late) and meal times.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ich esse spät zu Abend** - I eat dinner late
- **Das Essen ist in dreißig Minuten fertig** - The food will be ready in thirty minutes
- **Zum Abendessen** - For dinner
