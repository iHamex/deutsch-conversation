# Sportaktivitäten / Sports Activities

---

## 001: Fußball spielen / Playing Football

**Situation:**  
Anna spielt Fußball.  
*Anna plays football.*

### Dialog / Dialogue

**Anna:**  
Ich spiele gerne Fußball.  
*I like to play football.*

**Freundin:**  
Wo spielst du?  
*Where do you play?*

**Anna:**  
Im Park oder auf dem Platz.  
*In the park or on the field.*

**Freundin:**  
Wie oft spielst du?  
*How often do you play?*

**Anna:**  
Einmal pro Woche.  
*Once per week.*

**Freundin:**  
Spielst du in einem Verein?  
*Do you play in a club?*

**Anna:**  
Ja, ich bin in einem Verein.  
*Yes, I'm in a club.*

## Grammatik / Grammar

### Expressing "I play" with "ich spiele" (I play)

**English:** To express that you play a sport, use "ich spiele" (I play) followed by the sport name. This is the standard way to talk about playing sports.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ich spiele gerne Fußball** - I like to play football
- **Ich spiele Tennis** - I play tennis
- **Ich spiele Basketball** - I play basketball
- **Ich spiele Volleyball** - I play volleyball

### Asking "where" with "wo" (where)

**English:** To ask where someone plays a sport, use "wo" (where) followed by "spielst du" (do you play) or the appropriate verb. This helps understand locations.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Wo spielst du?** - Where do you play?
- **Wo schwimmst du?** - Where do you swim?
- **Wo fährst du?** - Where do you ride?
- **Im Park oder auf dem Platz** - In the park or on the field

### Expressing "I like" with "gerne" (gladly)

**English:** To express that you like doing a sport, add "gerne" (gladly) after the verb. This shows preference for activities.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ich spiele gerne Fußball** - I like to play football
- **Ich schwimme gerne** - I like to swim
- **Ich fahre gerne Rad** - I like to ride a bike
- **Ich fahre gerne Ski** - I like to ski

### Asking "how often" with "wie oft" (how often)

**English:** To ask how often someone does a sport, use "wie oft" (how often) followed by the verb. This helps understand frequency.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Wie oft spielst du?** - How often do you play?
- **Wie oft fährst du?** - How often do you go?
- **Einmal pro Woche** - Once per week
- **Zweimal pro Woche** - Twice per week
