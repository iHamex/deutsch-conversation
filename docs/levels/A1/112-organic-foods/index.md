# Bio-Lebensmittel / Organic Foods

---

## 001: Bio-Gemüse kaufen / Buying Organic Vegetables

**Situation:**  
Anna kauft Bio-Gemüse.  
*Anna buys organic vegetables.*

### Dialog / Dialogue

**Anna:**  
Entschuldigung, wo ist das Bio-Gemüse?  
*Excuse me, where is the organic vegetables?*

**Verkäufer:**  
Das Bio-Gemüse ist in Regal eins.  
*The organic vegetables are in aisle one.*

**Anna:**  
Danke!  
*Thanks!*

**Anna:**  
Welches Gemüse haben Sie?  
*Which vegetables do you have?*

**Verkäufer:**  
Wir haben verschiedene Bio-Gemüsesorten.  
*We have different organic vegetables.*

**Anna:**  
Sind sie teurer?  
*Are they more expensive?*

**Verkäufer:**  
Ja, etwas teurer, aber gesünder.  
*Yes, a bit more expensive, but healthier.*

**Anna:**  
Ich nehme Bio-Tomaten.  
*I'll take organic tomatoes.*

## Grammatik / Grammar

### Asking "Where" with "wo"

**English:** To ask where organic foods are located in a store, use "wo" (where) followed by "ist" (is) for singular items.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Wo ist das Bio-Gemüse?** - Where is the organic vegetables?
- **Das Bio-Gemüse ist in Regal eins** - The organic vegetables are in aisle one
- **Hier ist Bio-Obst** - Here is organic fruit
- **Hier ist Bio-Milch** - Here is organic milk

### Asking "What" with "was ist"

**English:** To ask what organic products are, use "was ist" (what is) followed by the item. This is useful when learning about organic foods.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Was ist Bio-Milch?** - What is organic milk?
- **Was ist Bio-Müsli?** - What is organic muesli?
- **Das ist Milch von Bio-Kühen** - That's milk from organic cows
- **Das ist Müsli aus Bio-Zutaten** - That's muesli made from organic ingredients

### Asking About Price with "wie viel kostet"

**English:** To ask how much organic products cost, use "wie viel kostet" (how much does ... cost) followed by the item. Note that organic products are often more expensive.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Wie viel kosten die Bio-Äpfel?** - How much do the organic apples cost?
- **Wie viel kostet ein Brot?** - How much does a bread cost?
- **Vier Euro pro Kilo** - Four euros per kilo
- **Das ist teuer** - That's expensive

### Expressing Reasons with "aber"

**English:** To give reasons why organic products might be worth the higher price, use "aber" (but) followed by the benefit. This is common when discussing organic vs. conventional products.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ja, etwas teurer, aber gesünder** - Yes, a bit more expensive, but healthier
- **Ja, aber sie sind ohne Pestizide** - Yes, but they are without pesticides
- **Ja, aber die Tiere leben besser** - Yes, but the animals live better
- **Ja, aber es ist gesünder** - Yes, but it's healthier
