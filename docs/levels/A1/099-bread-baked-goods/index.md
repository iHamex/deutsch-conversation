# Brot und Backwaren / Bread and Baked Goods

---

## 001: Brot kaufen / Buying Bread

**Situation:**  
Anna kauft Brot in der Bäckerei.  
*Anna buys bread at the bakery.*

### Dialog / Dialogue

**Anna:**  
Ich möchte Brot, bitte.  
*I would like bread, please.*

**Bäcker:**  
Gerne. Welches Brot möchten Sie?  
*Gladly. Which bread would you like?*

**Anna:**  
Was haben Sie?  
*What do you have?*

**Bäcker:**  
Wir haben Weißbrot, Vollkornbrot und Roggenbrot.  
*We have white bread, whole grain bread and rye bread.*

**Anna:**  
Ich nehme Vollkornbrot, bitte.  
*I'll take whole grain bread, please.*

**Bäcker:**  
Sehr gut. Ein ganzes Brot oder ein halbes?  
*Very good. A whole bread or a half?*

**Anna:**  
Ein halbes Brot, bitte.  
*A half bread, please.*

**Bäcker:**  
Das macht zwei Euro fünfzig.  
*That makes two euros fifty.*

## Grammatik / Grammar

### Bread and Baked Goods Names as Nouns

**English:** All bread and baked goods names in German are nouns and are capitalized. They often have specific articles (der/die/das).

**Examples from conversations:**

- **das Brot** - the bread
- **das Brötchen** - the roll
- **der Kuchen** - the cake
- **das Croissant** - the croissant

### Quantity Expressions: "Stück" and "ganz/halb"

**English:** "Stück" (piece) is used for individual items, and "ganz" (whole) and "halb" (half) for bread.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Vier Stück** - Four pieces
- **Ein ganzes Brot** - A whole bread
- **Ein halbes Brot** - A half bread
- **Zwei Stück** - Two pieces

### Types of Bread

**English:** German has specific terms for different types of bread, often using compound nouns.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Weißbrot** - White bread
- **Vollkornbrot** - Whole grain bread
- **Roggenbrot** - Rye bread
- **Sesambrötchen** - Sesame rolls

### "frisch" (fresh)

**English:** "Frisch" (fresh) is essential when buying bread and baked goods.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ist das Brot frisch?** - Is the bread fresh?
- **Ja, sehr frisch** - Yes, very fresh
- **Frisches Brot** - Fresh bread
