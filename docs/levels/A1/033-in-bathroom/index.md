# Im Badezimmer / In the Bathroom

---

## 001: Zähne putzen / Brushing Teeth

**Situation:**  
Maria putzt sich die Zähne.  
*Maria brushes her teeth.*

### Dialog / Dialogue

**Lisa:**  
Was machst du?  
*What are you doing?*

**Maria:**  
Ich putze mir die Zähne.  
*I'm brushing my teeth.*

**Lisa:**  
Wo ist deine Zahnbürste?  
*Where is your toothbrush?*

**Maria:**  
Im Badezimmer, im Schrank.  
*In the bathroom, in the cabinet.*

**Lisa:**  
Hast du auch Zahnpasta?  
*Do you also have toothpaste?*

**Maria:**  
Ja, natürlich.  
*Yes, of course.*

**Lisa:**  
Wie oft putzt du dir die Zähne?  
*How often do you brush your teeth?*

**Maria:**  
Zwei Mal am Tag.  
*Twice a day.*

**Lisa:**  
Das ist gut.  
*That's good.*

## Grammatik / Grammar

### Reflexive Verbs for Personal Care: "sich waschen", "sich putzen"

**English:** Many bathroom activities use reflexive verbs with "sich" (oneself): "sich waschen" (to wash oneself), "sich putzen" (to brush/clean oneself). These verbs show you're doing something to yourself.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ich putze mir die Zähne** - I brush my teeth
- **Ich wasche mir die Hände** - I wash my hands
- **Ich wasche mir die Haare** - I wash my hair
- **Ich rasiere mich** - I shave

### Asking "Where" with "wo" for Bathroom Items

**English:** When looking for bathroom items, use "wo ist" (where is) or "wo sind" (where are) to ask for their location. This helps find things in the bathroom.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Wo ist deine Zahnbürste?** - Where is your toothbrush?
- **Wo sind die Handtücher?** - Where are the towels?
- **Im Badezimmer, im Schrank** - In the bathroom, in the cabinet
- **Im Schrank im Badezimmer** - In the cabinet in the bathroom

### Frequency Expressions: "wie oft", "zwei Mal"

**English:** To ask or tell how often you do bathroom activities, use "wie oft" (how often) and frequency expressions like "zwei Mal am Tag" (twice a day) or "jeden Morgen" (every morning).

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Wie oft putzt du dir die Zähne?** - How often do you brush your teeth?
- **Zwei Mal am Tag** - Twice a day
- **Jeden Morgen** - Every morning
- **Zwei Mal pro Woche** - Twice a week

### Bathroom Items with Articles

**English:** Common bathroom items have specific articles: "die Zahnbürste" (the toothbrush), "die Zahnpasta" (the toothpaste), "das Handtuch" (the towel), "der Spiegel" (the mirror). These must be learned with each noun.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Hast du Zahnpasta?** - Do you have toothpaste?
- **Ich brauche ein Handtuch** - I need a towel
- **Der Spiegel ist sehr groß** - The mirror is very big
- **Ein großes Handtuch** - A big towel
