# Wochenendaktivitäten / Weekend Activities

---

## 001: Wochenendpläne / Weekend Plans

**Situation:**  
Zwei Freunde sprechen über ihre Wochenendpläne.  
*Two friends talk about their weekend plans.*

### Dialog / Dialogue

**Anna:**  
Was machst du am Wochenende?  
*What are you doing on the weekend?*

**Tom:**  
Am Samstag gehe ich einkaufen.  
*On Saturday I'm going shopping.*

**Anna:**  
Das klingt gut!  
*That sounds good!*

**Tom:**  
Und am Sonntag treffe ich Freunde.  
*And on Sunday I'm meeting friends.*

**Anna:**  
Das ist schön!  
*That's nice!*

**Tom:**  
Und du?  
*And you?*

**Anna:**  
Am Wochenende bleibe ich zu Hause.  
*On the weekend I'm staying at home.*

**Tom:**  
Das klingt entspannend!  
*That sounds relaxing!*

## Grammatik / Grammar

### Weekend Activities Verbs

**English:** Common verbs for weekend activities include: einkaufen gehen (to go shopping), Freunde treffen (to meet friends), spazieren gehen (to go for a walk), Sport machen (to do sports), ins Kino gehen (to go to the cinema), ins Restaurant gehen (to go to a restaurant), zu Hause bleiben (to stay at home), einen Ausflug machen (to go on an outing).

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ich gehe am Samstag einkaufen** - I'm going shopping on Saturday
- **Ich treffe Freunde** - I'm meeting friends
- **Ich gehe spazieren** - I'm going for a walk
- **Ich bleibe zu Hause** - I'm staying at home

### "Am Wochenende" (on the weekend)

**English:** "Am Wochenende" means "on the weekend" and is used to talk about weekend activities.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Was machst du am Wochenende?** - What are you doing on the weekend?
- **Am Wochenende gehe ich einkaufen** - On the weekend I'm going shopping
- **Am Samstag** - On Saturday
- **Am Sonntag** - On Sunday

### Separable Verbs: "einkaufen gehen", "spazieren gehen"

**English:** Many weekend activity verbs are separable. The prefix goes to the end of the sentence in main clauses.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ich gehe einkaufen** - I'm going shopping
- **Ich gehe spazieren** - I'm going for a walk
- **Ich gehe ins Kino** - I'm going to the cinema

### "Vielleicht" (maybe) for Uncertain Plans

**English:** "Vielleicht" (maybe) is used to express uncertainty about weekend plans.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Vielleicht gehe ich am Samstag ins Kino** - Maybe I'll go to the cinema on Saturday
- **Ich gehe vielleicht ins Restaurant** - Maybe I'll go to a restaurant
