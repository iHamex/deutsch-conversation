# Diätbeschränkungen / Dietary Restrictions

---

## 001: Glutenfreie Produkte / Gluten-Free Products

**Situation:**  
Anna fragt nach glutenfreien Produkten.  
*Anna asks about gluten-free products.*

### Dialog / Dialogue

**Anna:**  
Haben Sie glutenfreie Produkte?  
*Do you have gluten-free products?*

**Verkäufer:**  
Ja, wir haben glutenfreie Produkte.  
*Yes, we have gluten-free products.*

**Anna:**  
Welche Produkte haben Sie?  
*Which products do you have?*

**Verkäufer:**  
Wir haben glutenfreies Brot und Nudeln.  
*We have gluten-free bread and pasta.*

**Anna:**  
Wo sind sie?  
*Where are they?*

**Verkäufer:**  
In Regal zwei.  
*In aisle two.*

**Anna:**  
Danke!  
*Thanks!*

**Verkäufer:**  
Gern geschehen!  
*You're welcome!*

**Anna:**  
Ich nehme glutenfreies Brot.  
*I'll take gluten-free bread.*

## Grammatik / Grammar

### Asking About Availability with "haben Sie"

**English:** To ask if a store has products for specific dietary restrictions, use "haben Sie" (do you have) followed by the type of product. This is essential when shopping with dietary needs.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Haben Sie glutenfreie Produkte?** - Do you have gluten-free products?
- **Haben Sie laktosefreie Milch?** - Do you have lactose-free milk?
- **Haben Sie zuckerfreie Produkte?** - Do you have sugar-free products?
- **Ja, wir haben glutenfreie Produkte** - Yes, we have gluten-free products

### Asking "What" with "was ist"

**English:** To ask what special diet products are, use "was ist" (what is) followed by the item. This helps understand unfamiliar dietary products.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Was ist laktosefreie Milch?** - What is lactose-free milk?
- **Was ist das?** - What is that?
- **Das ist Milch ohne Laktose** - That's milk without lactose
- **Das sind Produkte ohne Zucker** - Those are products without sugar

### Asking About Price with "wie viel kostet"

**English:** To ask how much diet products cost, use "wie viel kostet" (how much does ... cost). Note that special diet products are often more expensive.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Wie viel kostet sie?** - How much does it cost?
- **Wie viel kosten sie?** - How much do they cost?
- **Zwei Euro pro Liter** - Two euros per liter
- **Etwas teurer als normale Produkte** - A bit more expensive than normal products

### Expressing Need with "ich brauche"

**English:** To express that you need specific diet products, use "ich brauche" (I need) followed by the items. This is important when shopping with dietary restrictions.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ich brauche verschiedene Diätprodukte** - I need different diet products
- **Ich brauche glutenfreie Produkte** - I need gluten-free products
- **Welche Produkte möchten Sie?** - Which products would you like?
- **Glutenfreies Brot und laktosefreie Milch** - Gluten-free bread and lactose-free milk
