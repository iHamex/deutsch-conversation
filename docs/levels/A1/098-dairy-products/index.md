# Milchprodukte / Dairy Products

---

## 001: Milch kaufen / Buying Milk

**Situation:**  
Anna kauft Milch im Supermarkt.  
*Anna buys milk at the supermarket.*

### Dialog / Dialogue

**Anna:**  
Ich möchte Milch, bitte.  
*I would like milk, please.*

**Verkäufer:**  
Gerne. Welche Milch möchten Sie?  
*Gladly. Which milk would you like?*

**Anna:**  
Was haben Sie?  
*What do you have?*

**Verkäufer:**  
Wir haben Vollmilch, fettarme Milch und H-Milch.  
*We have whole milk, low-fat milk and UHT milk.*

**Anna:**  
Ich nehme Vollmilch, bitte.  
*I'll take whole milk, please.*

**Verkäufer:**  
Sehr gut. Ein Liter?  
*Very good. One liter?*

**Anna:**  
Ja, bitte.  
*Yes, please.*

**Verkäufer:**  
Das macht einen Euro fünfzig.  
*That makes one euro fifty.*

## 010: Milchprodukte im Kühlschrank / Dairy Products in Refrigerator

**Situation:**  
Eine Familie spricht über Milchprodukte im Kühlschrank.  
*A family talks about dairy products in the refrigerator.*

### Dialog / Dialogue

**Mutter:**  
Was haben wir im Kühlschrank?  
*What do we have in the refrigerator?*

**Vater:**  
Wir haben Milch, Butter, Käse und Joghurt.  
*We have milk, butter, cheese and yogurt.*

**Mutter:**  
Gut. Brauchen wir noch etwas?  
*Good. Do we need anything else?*

**Vater:**  
Ja, wir brauchen noch Sahne.  
*Yes, we still need cream.*

**Mutter:**  
Gut. Ich kaufe Sahne morgen.  
*Good. I'll buy cream tomorrow.*

**Vater:**  
Danke.  
*Thank you.*

## Grammatik / Grammar

### Dairy Product Names as Nouns

**English:** All dairy product names in German are nouns and are capitalized. They often have specific articles (der/die/das).

**Examples from conversations:**

- **die Milch** - the milk
- **der Käse** - the cheese
- **der Joghurt** - the yogurt
- **die Butter** - the butter

### Quantity Expressions: "Liter" and "Gramm"

**English:** "Liter" (liter) and "Gramm" (gram) are used to specify quantities when buying dairy products.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ein Liter Milch** - One liter of milk
- **Zweihundert Gramm Käse** - Two hundred grams of cheese
- **Vier Becher** - Four cups

### Types of Dairy Products

**English:** German has specific terms for different types of dairy products, often using compound nouns.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Vollmilch** - Whole milk
- **Naturjoghurt** - Plain yogurt
- **Magerquark** - Low-fat quark
- **Kräuter-Frischkäse** - Herb cream cheese

### "brauchen" for Needs

**English:** "Brauchen" (to need) is used when talking about what dairy products you need.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ich brauche Milchprodukte** - I need dairy products
- **Was brauchen wir?** - What do we need?
- **Wir brauchen noch Sahne** - We still need cream
