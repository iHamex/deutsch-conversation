# Nachspeisen / Desserts

---

## 001: Kuchen / Cake

**Situation:**  
Anna bestellt ein Stück Kuchen als Nachspeise.  
*Anna orders a piece of cake as dessert.*

### Dialog / Dialogue

**Kellner:**  
Möchten Sie auch eine Nachspeise?  
*Would you also like a dessert?*

**Anna:**  
Ja, gerne. Was haben Sie?  
*Yes, gladly. What do you have?*

**Kellner:**  
Wir haben verschiedene Kuchen: Apfelkuchen, Schokoladenkuchen und Käsekuchen.  
*We have various cakes: apple cake, chocolate cake and cheesecake.*

**Anna:**  
Ich nehme ein Stück Apfelkuchen, bitte.  
*I'll take a piece of apple cake, please.*

**Kellner:**  
Sehr gut. Möchten Sie auch Sahne dazu?  
*Very good. Would you also like cream with it?*

**Anna:**  
Ja, bitte.  
*Yes, please.*

**Kellner:**  
Gut. Kommt sofort.  
*Good. Coming right away.*

## Grammatik / Grammar

### "als Nachspeise" (as dessert)

**English:** "Als Nachspeise" means "as dessert" and is used to specify when you eat something.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ich möchte eine Nachspeise** - I would like a dessert
- **Als Nachspeise esse ich Kuchen** - As dessert I eat cake
- **Möchten Sie auch eine Nachspeise?** - Would you also like a dessert?

### "satt sein" (to be full)

**English:** "Satt sein" means "to be full" and is commonly used when declining dessert.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ich bin satt** - I'm full
- **Ich bin schon satt** - I'm already full
- **Bist du satt?** - Are you full?

### Dessert Names as Nouns

**English:** All dessert names in German are nouns and are capitalized. They often have specific articles (der/die/das).

**Examples from conversations:**

- **der Kuchen** - the cake
- **das Eis** - the ice cream
- **der Pudding** - the pudding
- **der Obstsalat** - the fruit salad

### "auch" for Additional Items

**English:** "Auch" (also) is used when ordering additional items like cream or coffee with dessert.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Möchten Sie auch Sahne?** - Would you also like cream?
- **Ich möchte auch einen Kaffee** - I would also like a coffee
- **Auch gerne** - Also gladly
