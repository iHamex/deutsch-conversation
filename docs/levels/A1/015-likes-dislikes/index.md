# Vorlieben und Abneigungen / Likes and Dislikes

---

## 001: Mögen / To Like

**Situation:**  
Ein Lehrer erklärt, wie man Vorlieben ausdrückt.  
*A teacher explains how to express preferences.*

### Dialog / Dialogue

**Lehrerin:**  
Heute lernen wir, Vorlieben auszudrücken.  
*Today we learn to express preferences.*

**Schüler:**  
Wie sagt man "Ich mag etwas"?  
*How do you say "I like something"?*

**Lehrerin:**  
"Ich mag" bedeutet "I like".  
*"Ich mag" means "I like".*

**Schüler:**  
Ich mag Schokolade.  
*I like chocolate.*

**Lehrerin:**  
Richtig! Und wie sagt man "Ich mag nicht"?  
*Correct! And how do you say "I don't like"?*

**Schüler:**  
"Ich mag nicht" bedeutet "I don't like".  
*"Ich mag nicht" means "I don't like".*

**Lehrerin:**  
Sehr gut!  
*Very good!*

*I don't like horror movies.*

*I understand that.*

## Grammatik / Grammar

### "Mögen" (to like)

**English:** "Mögen" is used to express likes and preferences. It's conjugated: ich mag, du magst, er/sie/es mag, wir mögen, ihr mögt, sie/Sie mögen.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ich mag Pizza** - I like pizza
- **Ich mag Musik sehr** - I like music very much
- **Magst du Filme?** - Do you like movies?
- **Ich mag keinen Fisch** - I don't like fish

### "Gern" (gladly/like to)

**English:** "Gern" is used with verbs to express that you like doing something. It comes after the verb. "Ich lese gerne" means "I like to read" or "I read gladly".

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ich spiele gerne Fußball** - I like to play soccer
- **Ich lese gerne Bücher** - I like to read books
- **Was machst du gerne?** - What do you like to do?

### "Lieber" (prefer)

**English:** "Lieber" means "prefer" or "rather" and is used to express preference between two options.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ich mag Kaffee lieber** - I prefer coffee
- **Magst du Kaffee oder Tee lieber?** - Do you prefer coffee or tea?
- **Ich mag Tee lieber** - I prefer tea

### "Lieblings-" (favorite)

**English:** "Lieblings-" is a prefix used to form compound nouns meaning "favorite". It's combined with the noun: Lieblingsessen (favorite food), Lieblingsgetränk (favorite drink), Lieblingsfilm (favorite movie).

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Was ist dein Lieblingsessen?** - What is your favorite food?
- **Mein Lieblingsessen ist Pizza** - My favorite food is pizza
- **Mein Lieblingsgetränk ist Kaffee** - My favorite drink is coffee
