# Vegane Optionen / Vegan Options

---

## 001: Veganes Gericht bestellen / Ordering Vegan Dish

**Situation:**  
Anna bestellt ein veganes Gericht im Restaurant.  
*Anna orders a vegan dish at the restaurant.*

### Dialog / Dialogue

**Anna:**  
Haben Sie vegane Gerichte?  
*Do you have vegan dishes?*

**Kellner:**  
Ja, wir haben vegane Gerichte.  
*Yes, we have vegan dishes.*

**Anna:**  
Welche veganen Gerichte haben Sie?  
*Which vegan dishes do you have?*

**Kellner:**  
Wir haben vegane Pasta und vegane Salate.  
*We have vegan pasta and vegan salads.*

**Anna:**  
Was ist in der veganen Pasta?  
*What is in the vegan pasta?*

**Kellner:**  
Pasta mit Gemüse und Tomatensauce.  
*Pasta with vegetables and tomato sauce.*

**Anna:**  
Ist da Käse drin?  
*Is there cheese in it?*

**Kellner:**  
Nein, kein Käse.  
*No, no cheese.*

**Anna:**  
Perfekt! Ich nehme die vegane Pasta.  
*Perfect! I'll take the vegan pasta.*

## 002: Nach veganen Optionen fragen / Asking About Vegan Options

**Situation:**  
Tom fragt nach veganen Optionen.  
*Tom asks about vegan options.*

### Dialog / Dialogue

**Tom:**  
Entschuldigung, sind Sie vegan?  
*Excuse me, are you vegan?*

**Kellnerin:**  
Nein, aber wir haben vegane Gerichte.  
*No, but we have vegan dishes.*

**Tom:**  
Was bedeutet vegan?  
*What does vegan mean?*

**Kellnerin:**  
Vegan bedeutet ohne tierische Produkte.  
*Vegan means without animal products.*

**Tom:**  
Also kein Fleisch, kein Käse, keine Milch?  
*So no meat, no cheese, no milk?*

**Kellnerin:**  
Genau, nichts von Tieren.  
*Exactly, nothing from animals.*

**Tom:**  
Welche Gerichte sind vegan?  
*Which dishes are vegan?*

**Kellnerin:**  
Das Gemüsecurry ist vegan.  
*The vegetable curry is vegan.*

**Tom:**  
Gut, ich nehme das.  
*Good, I'll take that.*

## Grammatik / Grammar

### Asking "do you have" with "haben Sie" (do you have)

**English:** To ask if a restaurant has vegan options, use "haben Sie" (do you have) followed by the item. This is a common way to inquire about vegan menu items.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Haben Sie vegane Gerichte?** - Do you have vegan dishes?
- **Haben Sie vegane Pizza?** - Do you have vegan pizza?
- **Haben Sie veganes Frühstück?** - Do you have vegan breakfast?
- **Haben Sie vegane Suppe?** - Do you have vegan soup?

### Expressing "no" with "kein" (no)

**English:** To confirm that something doesn't contain animal products, use "kein" (no) followed by the item. This is essential when asking about vegan options.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Nein, kein Käse** - No, no cheese
- **Nein, keine Milch** - No, no milk
- **Kein Käse?** - No cheese?
- **Keine tierischen Produkte** - No animal products

### Asking "what does it mean" with "was bedeutet" (what does it mean)

**English:** To ask what "vegan" means, use "was bedeutet" (what does it mean) followed by the word. This helps understand dietary restrictions.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Was bedeutet vegan?** - What does vegan mean?
- **Vegan bedeutet ohne tierische Produkte** - Vegan means without animal products
- **Also kein Fleisch, kein Käse, keine Milch?** - So no meat, no cheese, no milk?
- **Genau, nichts von Tieren** - Exactly, nothing from animals

### Asking "is there" with "ist da" (is there)

**English:** To ask if something contains animal products, use "ist da" (is there) followed by the item and "drin" (in it). This helps verify vegan options.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ist da Käse drin?** - Is there cheese in it?
- **Ist da Milch drin?** - Is there milk in it?
- **Kein Käse?** - No cheese?
- **Nein, kein Käse** - No, no cheese
