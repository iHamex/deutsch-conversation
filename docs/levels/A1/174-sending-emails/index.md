# E-Mails senden / Sending Emails

---

## 001: E-Mail schreiben / Writing Email

**Situation:**  
Anna schreibt eine E-Mail.  
*Anna writes an email.*

### Dialog / Dialogue

**Anna:**  
Ich muss eine E-Mail schreiben.  
*I need to write an email.*

**Kollege:**  
An wen schreibst du?  
*Who are you writing to?*

**Anna:**  
An meinen Chef.  
*To my boss.*

**Kollege:**  
Was schreibst du?  
*What are you writing?*

**Anna:**  
Ich schreibe über das Meeting.  
*I'm writing about the meeting.*

**Kollege:**  
Das ist wichtig.  
*That's important.*

**Anna:**  
Ja, ich schreibe es jetzt.  
*Yes, I'm writing it now.*

## Grammatik / Grammar

### Expressing "I need to write" with "ich muss schreiben" (I need to write)

**English:** To express that you need to write an email, use "ich muss eine E-Mail schreiben" (I need to write an email). This initiates email communication.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ich muss eine E-Mail schreiben** - I need to write an email
- **Ich schreibe eine E-Mail** - I'm writing an email
- **An wen schreibst du?** - Who are you writing to?
- **Was schreibst du?** - What are you writing?

### Expressing "to whom" with "an wen" (to whom)

**English:** To ask who someone is writing an email to, use "an wen" (to whom) followed by "schreibst du" (are you writing). This identifies the recipient.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **An wen schreibst du?** - Who are you writing to?
- **An meinen Chef** - To my boss
- **An Maria** - To Maria
- **An wen?** - To whom?

### Expressing "I send" with "ich sende" (I send)

**English:** To express that you're sending an email, use "ich sende" (I send) or "ich sende die E-Mail" (I'm sending the email). This completes the action.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ich sende die E-Mail** - I'm sending the email
- **Dann sende die E-Mail** - Then send the email
- **Ich sende sie jetzt** - I'm sending it now
- **Drücken Sie auf "Senden"** - Press "Send"

### Expressing "I receive" with "ich erhalte" or "ich habe" (I receive/I have)

**English:** To express that you received an email, use "ich habe eine E-Mail" (I have an email) or "ich erhalte" (I receive). This indicates incoming mail.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ich habe eine neue E-Mail** - I have a new email
- **Von wem ist die E-Mail?** - Who is the email from?
- **Was steht drin?** - What's in it?
- **Von meinem Chef** - From my boss
