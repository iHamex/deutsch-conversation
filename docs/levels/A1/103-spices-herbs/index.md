# Gewürze und Kräuter / Spices and Herbs

---

## 001: Salz und Pfeffer kaufen / Buying Salt and Pepper

**Situation:**  
Anna kauft Salz und Pfeffer im Supermarkt.  
*Anna buys salt and pepper at the supermarket.*

### Dialog / Dialogue

**Anna:**  
Entschuldigung, wo ist das Salz?  
*Excuse me, where is the salt?*

**Verkäufer:**  
Das Salz ist in Regal zwei.  
*The salt is in aisle two.*

**Anna:**  
Danke!  
*Thanks!*

**Anna:**  
Ich suche auch Pfeffer.  
*I'm also looking for pepper.*

**Verkäufer:**  
Der Pfeffer ist neben dem Salz.  
*The pepper is next to the salt.*

**Anna:**  
Perfekt!  
*Perfect!*

**Anna:**  
Wie viel kostet das Salz?  
*How much does the salt cost?*

**Verkäufer:**  
Ein Euro fünfzig.  
*One euro fifty.*

**Anna:**  
Und der Pfeffer?  
*And the pepper?*

**Verkäufer:**  
Zwei Euro.  
*Two euros.*

## Grammatik / Grammar

### Asking "Where" with "wo"

**English:** To ask where something is located, use "wo" (where) followed by "ist" (is) for singular or "sind" (are) for plural. This is useful when shopping for spices and herbs.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Wo ist das Salz?** - Where is the salt?
- **Wo sind die Zwiebeln?** - Where are the onions?
- **Das Salz ist in Regal zwei** - The salt is in aisle two
- **Die Zwiebeln sind hier** - The onions are here

### Asking About Availability with "haben Sie"

**English:** To ask if a shop has specific spices or herbs, use "haben Sie" (do you have) followed by the item. The answer is "ja" (yes) or "nein" (no).

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Haben Sie frische Kräuter?** - Do you have fresh herbs?
- **Haben Sie Paprika?** - Do you have paprika?
- **Haben Sie auch Vanille?** - Do you also have vanilla?
- **Ja, wir haben viele Kräuter** - Yes, we have many herbs

### Asking "Which" with "welche/welcher/welches"

**English:** When choosing between different spices or herbs, use "welche" (which - feminine/plural), "welcher" (which - masculine), or "welches" (which - neuter) followed by the noun.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Welche Kräuter haben Sie?** - Which herbs do you have?
- **Welche Mischungen haben Sie?** - Which mixes do you have?
- **Welche sind schärfer?** - Which ones are spicier?
- **Welchen Paprika möchten Sie?** - Which paprika would you like?

### Expressing Need with "ich brauche"

**English:** To express that you need something, use "ich brauche" (I need) followed by the item. This is useful when shopping for specific ingredients.

**Examples from conversations:**

- **Ich brauche Basilikum** - I need basil
- **Ich brauche Rosmarin und Thymian** - I need rosemary and thyme
- **Ich suche Zimt** - I'm looking for cinnamon
- **Ich suche auch Pfeffer** - I'm also looking for pepper
